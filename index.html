Panchsheel Prime 390 Overview

Panchsheel has launched its premium housing project, Prime 390, in Opp. Govindpuram Anaaj Mandi, Ghaziabad. The project offers 3 BHK Apartment of 1640 sqft in Ghaziabad Central. Among the many luxurious amenities that the project hosts are Walking Track, Club House, Intercom etc. These well-appointed low-rise floors are part of Primrose, a modern gated residential project already a home to many families. Here you get to enjoy the facilities and amenities of group housing without having to compromise on anything. Come, indulge in an independent lifestyle that gives you the freedom of privacy and living as per your own choice.

Low-rise floors with the facilities of a group housing project • G+4, Two side open 3 BHK Floors, • Shopping convenience for daily needs • Parks with play area, walking & jogging tracks • Spacious and well ventilated rooms with windows open to fresh air • Rain water harvesting • Reserved car parking • Power back up facility • 24hrs. water supply in bathrooms & kitchen • Dedicated lift for each tower • In house club with modern facilities - Round the clock security with intercom.
